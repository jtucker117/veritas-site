What's Included

Roof work, done right the first time.

Texas weather is hard on roofs — wind, hail, sun, and heavy rain. We do the prep right so the roof lasts, not just the shingle warranty.

01

Tear-Off & Decking

Complete tear-off, decking inspection, replacement of any rotten or soft sheathing, and a clean deck before underlayment goes down.

02

Underlayment & Flashing

Synthetic underlayment, ice-and-water shield at valleys and penetrations, properly stepped flashing at walls and chimneys — the things that prevent leaks.

03

Shingle, Metal, or Flat

Architectural asphalt shingle, standing-seam metal, or modified-bitumen flat roofing — material matched to your roof pitch, exposure, and budget.

04

Storm Damage & Insurance

Hail or wind hit your roof? We'll walk it with you, document the damage, and work with your insurance adjuster so the claim covers what should be covered.

How It Works

A clear path from inspection to handover.

1

Free Inspection

We climb the roof, take pictures, and walk you through what we find — including whether a repair or full replacement is the right call.

2

Estimate & Scheduling

Written line-item estimate with materials, labor, and a clear schedule. Insurance work coordinated with your adjuster.

3

Build

Tear-off and install typically in 1 to 3 days for a standard residential roof. Yard kept clean. Magnetic sweep at the end so no nails get left behind.

4

Final Walkthrough

We walk the roof and yard with you, hand over the warranty paperwork, and don't call it done until you do.
