What's Included

A full-scope bathroom build.

Demo, waterproofing, tile, glass, plumbing, electrical, paint, and trim — coordinated under one project lead so nothing falls through the cracks.

01

Showers, Tubs & Conversions

Walk-in tile showers with curbless options, tub-to-shower conversions, freestanding tubs, and glass enclosures sized to fit.

02

Tile & Waterproofing

Schluter Kerdi or Wedi waterproofing under every tile shower, proper slope to drains, sealed grout — the things that prevent leaks five years from now.

Custom or pre-built vanities, quartz or stone tops, undermount sinks, and fixture upgrades sourced and installed in-house.

Layout & Structural

How It Works

A clear path from idea to handover.

1

Walkthrough & Estimate

We meet at the bathroom, measure, talk through what isn't working, and follow up with a written line-item estimate.

2

Selections & Plan

Tile, fixtures, vanity, and layout finalized — then materials ordered and the build scheduled in writing.

3

Build

Demo, plumbing, waterproofing, tile, finish work, and punch list — typically 2 to 4 weeks for a single bathroom depending on scope.

4

Final Walkthrough

We pressure-test the shower, walk every detail with you, and don't call it done until you do.
