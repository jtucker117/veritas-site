What's Included

Design through finish — under one team.

Additions touch every trade. We handle the foundation, framing, mechanicals, and finishes so you have one accountable point of contact from the first sketch to the final walkthrough.

01

Design & Engineering

Plans drawn by our design partners, stamped engineering when the load path or foundation calls for it, and your input at every revision.

02

Permits & HOA

We pull permits with the city or county, and handle HOA architectural review submissions where required so your project moves on time.

03

Foundation & Framing

Slab, pier-and-beam, or matched foundation type for tie-in. Framing tied into the existing structure correctly — load paths verified before drywall.

04

Mechanicals & Finish

HVAC sized to the new square footage, electrical and plumbing, insulation, drywall, paint, flooring, and trim — all coordinated through one schedule.

How It Works

A clear path from idea to handover.

1

Walkthrough & Concept

We walk your house, talk through what space you actually need, and outline the realistic options — including what it'll cost in rough order of magnitude.

2

Plans & Estimate

Architectural plans drawn, engineering as needed, then a written line-item estimate against the final scope.

3

Build

Permits, foundation, framing, mechanicals, finishes, and punch list — typically 3 to 6 months for a typical room addition depending on scope.

4

Final Walkthrough

We walk every detail with you, fix anything that isn't right, and don't call it done until you do.
