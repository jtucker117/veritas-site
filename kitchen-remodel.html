What's Included

A full-scope kitchen build.

Whether you're refreshing a 1990s builder kitchen or knocking out a wall to open the floor plan, we cover the whole job under one accountable team.

01

Cabinetry & Counters

Custom or semi-custom cabinetry, drawer hardware, soft-close mechanisms, and stone or quartz countertops sized and installed in-house.

02

Tile, Flooring & Backsplash

Tile demo, prep, and install — backsplash, floor tile, or wood-look LVP — leveled and sealed to last.

03

Plumbing & Electrical

Relocate sinks, add islands, upgrade lighting, run dedicated circuits for ovens and microwaves — coordinated with our licensed sub trades.

04

Layout & Structural

Removing a wall to open up the kitchen? We handle the engineering, permits, and structural framing before any drywall touches the new opening.

How It Works

A clear path from idea to handover.

1

Walkthrough & Estimate

We meet you at the kitchen, measure, talk through what you want changed, and follow up with a written line-item estimate.

2

Selections & Plan

We help narrow down cabinets, counters, and finishes — then lock the plan, materials, and start date in writing.

3

Build

Demo, rough-ins, cabinets, tops, tile, paint, and punch list — managed by one project lead with daily updates.

4

Final Walkthrough

We walk every detail with you, fix anything that isn't right, and don't call it done until you do.
