About Veritas Ventures

Honest work — and we let the work speak.

Veritas Ventures is a Texas-based holding company headquartered in the Magnolia / Pinehurst area of Montgomery County. We bring together complementary ventures across construction, property management, and community impact — unified by a shared standard of integrity, quality, and long-term relationships.

Our flagship division, Veritas Builders, is a general contractor serving Greater Houston. As a newer, growing company, we’re structured to add divisions over time under one trusted name — earning business the right way: through honest pricing, quality work, and referrals.
